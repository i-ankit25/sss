Message

 Dear Parents and Students,

If you are looking for a well qualified and experienced Home Tutor or Coaching teacher who can make learning hard concepts an easy and a fun filled process, we'd be glad to help you. We are proud to have helped parents and students who want to bring out their child's maximum potential and get more out of education.

What we provide

Every Student is unique - differ in behavior, understanding skills and learning abilities. Some students learn quickly while some might need extra attention to bring out the best of their potential.Some students are comfortable with a classroom learning environment while some feel it to be a little restrictive and distracting. For the latter ones, home tutorial comes out to be a great option.That is why at Smartstudy Square we cater to all the students according to their needs.

Here is how we ensure to help you child :

1.Convenience for Learners :  The primary benefit is Convenience. We provide coaching classes as well as home tutions as per the convenience of the student. It provides the students and parents to choose what suits them and allows their child to learn in a environment of their choice. The students have a choice to learn in a competitve environment of a coaching class or in comfortable environment of his home.

2.Dedicated attention  :  At school, teachers are unable to focus on each and every student as a result some fall behind and find it hard to cope with others.But, here we understand that every student requires special attention and so we focus on each and every student. We make sure that your child is able to understand the topics aat their own pace.

3.Much smaller student to teacher ratio :  Because private tuition takes place in small group or one-to-one, students are able to focus better and are taught in a way that specifically meets their own unique needs.

4.Lesser distractions :  We provide an evironment where learners face fewer distractions in home environment or in coaching centre than in schools/colleges and other public spaces. It bring them more comfort, thereby allowing them to focus better. The sessions are also less restrictive and less formal than a school.

5.Finding the best learning style :  A lot of students often feel that learning in a school classroom is quite ineffective due to the restrictions of time and resources and distractions.
